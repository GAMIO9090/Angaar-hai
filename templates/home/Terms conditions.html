Terms & Conditions

Platform use karne se pehle yeh terms dhyan se padhein. Yeh agreement aap aur InfluConnect ke beech hai.

Effective: April 2026 ~8 min read
01

Terms Ki Acceptance

InfluConnect platform access ya use karke aap agree karte hain ki aap in Terms & Conditions se bound hain. Agar aap 18 saal se kam umra ke hain ya in terms se agree nahi karte, toh platform use mat karein.

Platform use karna = Terms se agreement. Hum kabhi bhi in terms ko update kar sakte hain. Continued use ka matlab hai aap updated terms se agree karte hain.

02

User Accounts

Platform use karne ke liye account banana zaroori hai. Account registration mein:

  • Accurate aur complete information provide karna aapki responsibility hai
  • Account credentials confidential rakhna zaroori hai
  • Ek person ek hi account rakh sakta hai
  • Account activity ki poori responsibility account owner ki hai
  • Suspicious activity hone pe turant hume inform karein

Hum accounts suspend ya terminate kar sakte hain agar terms violate hoti hain, bina prior notice ke.

03

Platform Services

InfluConnect provide karta hai:

  • Influencer discovery aur search platform
  • Booking management system
  • In-platform messaging between shopkeepers aur influencers
  • Campaign tracking aur reporting
  • Secure payment processing
  • AI-powered assistant for platform navigation

Service Availability: Hum 99% uptime ka target rakhte hain lekin guarantee nahi de sakte. Maintenance, updates, ya technical issues ke wajah se temporary downtime ho sakta hai. Critical bookings ke liye advance planning karein.

04

Booking Rules

Bookings ke liye yeh rules apply hote hain:

  • Shopkeeper booking request bhejta hai, influencer accept/reject karta hai
  • Confirmed booking ke baad cancellation policy apply hoti hai
  • Campaign details — deliverables, timeline, payment — booking mein clearly mention hongi
  • Dono parties ko agreed terms follow karne honge
  • Disputes ke liye platform ka mediation process use karna hoga

Cancellation: 48 hours se pehle cancel karne pe full refund. 24-48 hours mein 50% refund. 24 hours se kam mein no refund.

05

Payments & Fees

Payment structure:

  • Platform ke through secure payment required hai — direct payment prohibited
  • InfluConnect 10% platform fee charge karta hai each transaction pe
  • GST aur applicable taxes separately apply honge
  • Influencer ko campaign completion ke baad 3-5 business days mein payment milti hai
  • Disputed payments escrow mein hold hote hain resolution tak
06

User Conduct

Platform pe yeh strictly prohibited hai:

  • Fake profiles ya misleading information create karna
  • Spam, harassment, ya abusive behavior
  • Platform ke bahar direct deals karna (fees avoid karne ke liye)
  • Fake reviews ya ratings submit karna
  • Copyrighted content bina permission ke use karna
  • Platform security ko compromise karne ki koshish
  • Multiple accounts banana ek hi person ke liye

Violations pe immediate account suspension ho sakta hai aur legal action bhi possible hai.

07

Intellectual Property

Content ownership ke baare mein:

  • InfluConnect platform, logo, aur branding hamari property hai
  • User-generated content ka ownership creator ke paas rehta hai
  • Platform pe content upload karke aap hume limited license dete hain display ke liye
  • Campaign content ka ownership agreement mein specify hona chahiye
08

Limitation of Liability

InfluConnect liable nahi hai:

  • Users ke beech disputes ya unresolved transactions ke liye
  • Campaign results ya ROI guarantees ke liye
  • Third-party services se hone wale issues ke liye
  • Indirect ya consequential damages ke liye
  • Force majeure events (natural disasters, internet outages) ke liye

Hamare maximum liability limit ek transaction mein paid amount tak restricted hai, kabhi isse zyada nahi.

09

Termination

Account termination ke rules:

  • Aap kabhi bhi account delete kar sakte hain settings se
  • Hum terms violation pe bina notice account terminate kar sakte hain
  • Termination ke baad pending bookings complete ki jaayengi
  • Owed payments termination ke baad bhi process honge
  • Data deletion 90 days mein complete hogi (legal holds exclude)
