01 — Overview

Privacy Overview

ShopInfluence ("hum", "hamare", "platform") ek local business promotion platform hai jo shopkeepers ko nearby influencers se connect karta hai. Is Privacy Policy mein bataya gaya hai ki jab aap hamare platform ka use karte hain toh hum aapki kaunsi jaankari collect karte hain, uska kaise istemal karte hain aur use protect karne ke liye kya kadam uthate hain.

Jurisdiction Yeh policy India ke Information Technology Act, 2000 aur IT (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures) Rules, 2011 ke under governed hai.
02 — Collection

Data Collection

Hum do taraf se data collect karte hain:

🏪 Shopkeeper Data

Naam, shop address, GST number, category, contact details, payment info

📱 Influencer Data

Naam, social media handles, follower count, location, bank details, portfolio

Automatically collect kiya jaata hai:

  • Device information aur browser type
  • IP address aur location (approximate)
  • Platform usage patterns aur analytics
  • Transaction history
  • Communication logs (support queries)
03 — Usage

How We Use Your Data

Aapka data sirf in purposes ke liye use hota hai:

  • Account creation aur verification
  • Shopkeeper-Influencer matching algorithm improve karna
  • Payments process karna aur financial records maintain karna
  • Platform security aur fraud prevention
  • Customer support provide karna
  • Platform improvements ke liye anonymized analytics
  • Aapki consent se marketing communications
No Profiling Hum aapka data third-party advertising platforms ko nahi dete aur aapki online activity track nahi karte platform ke bahar.
04 — Sharing

Data Sharing

Aapka data sirf limited circumstances mein share hota hai:

  • Deal Partners: Jab aap koi deal accept karte hain, toh limited info dusre party ko visible hoti hai
  • Payment Processors: Secure transactions ke liye (Razorpay / PayU)
  • Legal Requirements: Government ya court order ke case mein
  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting, email service (NDA ke under)

Hum aapka data kabhi nahi sell, rent, ya exchange karte hain advertisers ko.

05 — Storage

Storage & Security

  • Data India-based servers par store hota hai
  • Passwords bcrypt encryption se store hote hain
  • Payment data PCI-DSS compliant processors ke through handle hota hai
  • Regular security audits aur penetration testing
  • Account deletion ke 90 days baad data permanently delete hota hai
06 — Cookies

Cookies & Tracking

Hum in cookies use karte hain:

  • Essential Cookies: Login session aur basic functionality ke liye (opt-out possible nahi)
  • Analytics Cookies: Platform usage samajhne ke liye (opt-out available)
  • Preference Cookies: Aapki settings yaad rakhne ke liye

Browser settings se aap cookies manage kar sakte hain.

07 — Rights

Your Rights

Aapke paas yeh rights hain:

  • Apna data access karne ka right
  • Galat information correct karwane ka right
  • Account aur data delete karne ka right
  • Marketing emails se unsubscribe karne ka right
  • Data portability — apna data download karne ka right
  • Automated decisions ko challenge karne ka right
How to Exercise In rights ke liye [email protected] par email karein. Hum 30 days mein respond karenge.
08 — Children

Children's Privacy

ShopInfluence sirf 18+ users ke liye hai. Hum jaanboojhkar 18 saal se kam umra ke kisi bhi vyakti ka data collect nahi karte. Agar hume pata chale ki koi minor ka account hai, toh use turant delete kar diya jaayega.

09 — Changes

Policy Changes

Hum is policy ko update kar sakte hain. Major changes ke liye hum aapko email ya in-app notification se inform karenge. Continued use of the platform after changes means acceptance of the updated policy.
