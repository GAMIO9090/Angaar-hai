Privacy Policy

Aapki privacy hamare liye sabse important hai. Yeh document batata hai ki hum aapka data kaise collect, use aur protect karte hain.

Last Updated: April 2026 ~5 min read
01

Introduction

InfluConnect ("hum", "humara", ya "platform") ek influencer marketing platform hai jo local businesses ko nearby content creators se connect karta hai. Yeh Privacy Policy explain karti hai ki jab aap hamari website influconnect.in use karte hain toh hum aapki personal information kaise handle karte hain.

Is platform ko use karke aap is Privacy Policy se agree karte hain. Agar aap agree nahi karte, toh kripya platform use karna band karein.

02

Data Jo Hum Collect Karte Hain

Hum alag-alag sources se information collect karte hain:

  • Account Information: Naam, email address, phone number, aur password jab aap register karte hain
  • Profile Data: Business ya influencer profile details, location, category, aur social media handles
  • Transaction Data: Booking history, payment information (encrypted), aur campaign details
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent, aur click patterns
  • Device Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, aur device identifiers
  • Location Data: City/area information jo aap provide karte hain influencer search ke liye
  • Communications: Messages jo aap platform pe bhejte hain, support tickets, aur feedback
03

Data Ka Use

Collected information ka use hum in purposes ke liye karte hain:

  • Platform services provide karna aur account manage karna
  • Shopkeepers ko relevant influencers se match karna
  • Booking aur payment transactions process karna
  • Customer support aur queries resolve karna
  • Platform security maintain karna aur fraud prevent karna
  • Service improvements ke liye analytics aur research
  • Legal obligations comply karna

Important: Hum aapka personal data kabhi third-party advertisers ko sell nahi karte. Aapka data sirf platform operations ke liye use hota hai.

04

Data Sharing

Hum limited circumstances mein data share karte hain:

  • Platform Users: Shopkeepers influencer profiles dekh sakte hain (public info only)
  • Service Providers: Payment processors, email services, hosting providers jo hamari behalf pe kaam karte hain
  • Legal Requirements: Agar court order ya government authority require kare
  • Business Transfer: Merger ya acquisition ke case mein, advance notice ke saath
05

Data Security

Aapki information protect karne ke liye hum industry-standard security measures use karte hain:

SSL/TLS Encryption — Saara data transmission encrypted hai

Password Hashing — Passwords kabhi plain text mein store nahi hote

Regular Audits — Security vulnerabilities ke liye regular checks

Access Controls — Sirf authorized personnel ko sensitive data access milta hai

Lekin koi bhi system 100% secure nahi hota. Agar aapko koi security issue lagta hai, turant security@influconnect.in pe report karein.

06

Aapke Rights

India ke IT Act aur applicable laws ke under aapke yeh rights hain:

  • Apna personal data access karne ka right
  • Galat information correct karwane ka right
  • Data deletion request karne ka right (kuch exceptions ke saath)
  • Marketing communications se opt-out karne ka right
  • Data portability — apna data download karne ka right

In rights exercise karne ke liye privacy@influconnect.in pe email karein. Hum 30 din ke andar respond karenge.

07

Cookies

Hum cookies aur similar technologies use karte hain:

  • Essential Cookies: Login session aur security ke liye zaroori
  • Analytics Cookies: Platform usage samajhne ke liye (Google Analytics)
  • Preference Cookies: Aapki language aur display preferences save karne ke liye

Browser settings se aap cookies disable kar sakte hain, lekin kuch features kaam nahi karenge.

08

Policy Changes

Hum is Privacy Policy ko update kar sakte hain. Significant changes ke liye hum email ya platform notification ke through inform karenge. Latest version hamesha is page pe available rahega.
