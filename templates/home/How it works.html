Frequently Asked Questions

Koi confusion? Yahan sabse common sawaalon ke jawab hain.

InfluConnect use karna kitna costly hai?

Registration aur browsing bilkul free hai. InfluConnect sirf successful bookings pe 10% platform fee charge karta hai. Koi monthly subscription nahi, koi hidden charges nahi.

Influencers ki authenticity kaise verify hoti hai?

Sare influencers ko social media profile verification, ID proof aur phone verification se guzarna padta hai. Fake followers wale accounts ko automatically detect karke remove kar diya jaata hai.

Agar influencer kaam nahi karta toh kya hoga?

Aapka payment escrow mein secure rehta hai jab tak campaign complete nahi hota. Agar influencer deliver nahi karta, full refund guaranteed hai. Humara dispute team 48 hours mein resolve karta hai.

Minimum followers kitne chahiye influencer banane ke liye?

Minimum 1,000 genuine followers chahiye kisi bhi platform pe. Micro-influencers (1K–10K) often better engagement rate rakhte hain compared to mega influencers. Quality over quantity!

Kya platform sirf certain cities mein available hai?

InfluConnect poore India mein available hai. Location-based search se aap apne exact area ke influencers dhundh sakte hain — chahe bada sheher ho ya chhota qasbah.
