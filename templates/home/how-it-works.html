FAQ

Common Sawaal

Kya main free mein join kar sakta hoon?
+

Haan! ShopInfluence bilkul free hai join karne ke liye — shopkeeper aur influencer dono ke liye. Sirf tab charge hota hai jab koi deal successfully complete hoti hai.

Influencer ko payment kab milegi?
+

Jab shopkeeper content approve kare, uske 24 hours ke andar payment directly influencer ke bank account mein transfer ho jaati hai. Payment escrow mein safe hoti hai.

Agar influencer ne kaam theek se nahi kiya toh?
+

Shopkeeper content reject kar sakta hai aur revision request kar sakta hai. Agar dispute banta hai, hamaari support team 24 hours mein intervene karti hai aur escrow funds hold mein rehte hain.

Kya chote influencers (micro-influencers) bhi join kar sakte hain?
+

Bilkul! 1000 followers se shuru karne wale micro-influencers bhi welcome hain. Actually local shops ke liye micro-influencers zyada effective hote hain kyunki unki audience local aur engaged hoti hai.

Kaise pata chalega ki influencer mere area mein hai?
+

Registration ke time aap apni location set karte hain. Platform automatically nearby influencers show karta hai — pin-code aur city level filtering available hai.
