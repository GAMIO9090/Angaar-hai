02 — Eligibility

Eligibility

Platform use karne ke liye aapko yeh criteria meet karne honge:

  • Aapki umar 18 saal ya usse zyada honi chahiye
  • India mein legally registered business ya individual hona chahiye
  • Valid phone number aur email address hona chahiye
  • Aap pehle se platform se permanently ban nahi hue hon
03 — User Roles

User Roles

Hamare platform par do tarah ke users hote hain:

  • Shopkeeper: Local business owner jo influencers se apni shop promote karwana chahte hain
  • Influencer: Content creator jo local businesses ke liye promotional content banata hai

Dono roles ki alag-alag responsibilities aur permissions hain. Ek account se dono roles simultaneously use karna allowed nahi hai.

04 — Conduct

Platform Conduct

Yeh kaam platform par strictly prohibited hain:

  • Fake reviews ya misleading promotional content post karna
  • Doosre users ko harass, threaten ya defame karna
  • Platform ko spam ya fraudulent activities ke liye use karna
  • Third-party intellectual property ka unauthorized use
  • Payment process ko manipulate karne ki koshish karna
  • Multiple fake accounts banana
Violation Consequence Kin bhi in rules ka violation permanent account ban aur legal action ka kaaran ban sakta hai.
05 — Payments

Payments & Fees

ShopInfluence ek commission-based model par operate karta hai:

  • Platform ek agreed deal ka 10% service fee charge karta hai
  • Payments platform ke through hi process hone chahiye — direct dealing prohibited hai
  • Influencer ko payment tab release hogi jab shopkeeper content approve kar de
  • Dispute case mein payment 7 working days hold ki ja sakti hai
  • Refund policy sirf platform ke fault ke case mein applicable hai
06 — Content

Content Policy

Sabhi promotional content ke liye yeh guidelines mandatory hain:

  • Content accurate aur misleading nahi hona chahiye
  • Paid promotion clearly disclose karna zaroori hai (#ad ya #sponsored)
  • Content ASCI (Advertising Standards Council of India) guidelines follow kare
  • Offensive, sexual ya hate speech wala content strictly banned hai
  • ShopInfluence ko content review aur remove karne ka adhikar hai
07 — IP

Intellectual Property

Platform par upload kiya gaya content ke baare mein:

  • Content ka copyright original creator ke paas rehta hai
  • Platform ko content display karne ka limited license milta hai
  • ShopInfluence ke logo, brand elements copy karna prohibited hai
  • Shopkeeper aur Influencer ke beech content rights deal ke time define hone chahiye
08 — Liability

Limitation of Liability

ShopInfluence ek intermediary platform hai. Hum responsible nahi hain:

  • Influencer ke content se shopkeeper ki reputation ko kisi bhi nuksaan ke liye
  • Deal ke outcome ya business results ke liye
  • Third-party payment gateway issues ke liye
  • Users ke beech kisi bhi personal disputes ke liye
09 — Termination

Account Termination

ShopInfluence kisi bhi account ko terminate kar sakta hai agar:

  • Terms of Service ka violation ho

Termination ke baad pending payments ko 14 working days mein settle kiya jaata hai.
